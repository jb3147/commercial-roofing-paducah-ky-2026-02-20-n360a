Core Service

Commercial Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and replacement for aging or failing commercial roofs, including flat, metal, and low-slope systems. We design code-compliant, manufacturer-approved assemblies that handle Paducah’s weather and protect your building for the long term.

Fast Response

Leak Repair & Emergency Service

Targeted repairs to stop active leaks, secure loose membranes, and address storm or impact damage. Our crews respond quickly in Paducah and nearby communities to protect inventory, equipment, and interiors from further water intrusion.

Life Extension

Roof Coatings & Restoration

Seamless elastomeric and reflective coatings for eligible commercial roofs to extend service life and improve energy performance. We repair problem areas, prepare the surface, and apply coating systems that reduce heat gain and future maintenance costs.

Proactive

Roof Inspections & Condition Reports

Detailed inspections with photo documentation, moisture checks, and clear recommendations. Ideal for property managers, owners, and lenders who need an objective assessment of current roof condition and planning for upcoming capital needs.

Preventative

Preventative Maintenance Programs

Scheduled maintenance plans that include cleaning drains, sealing penetrations, minor repairs, and routine inspections. Our programs are tailored to your roof type and business operations to reduce unexpected leaks and extend system life.

Specialty

Flat, Metal & Low-Slope Roof Systems

Design and installation of TPO, EPDM, PVC, and coated metal systems for warehouses, retail centers, medical offices, and more. We match materials and details to your building’s structure, drainage, and energy goals for dependable, long-term performance.
