About Paducah Commercial Roofing

A dedicated commercial roofing partner for businesses in Paducah, KY

Paducah Commercial Roofing was founded to bring specialized, commercial-only roofing expertise to businesses throughout Paducah, Kentucky. After years of working in both residential and commercial roofing, our founders saw a clear gap: commercial property owners needed a partner who fully understood the complexity of flat and low-slope systems, tenant coordination, and long-term asset planning. Today, our team focuses exclusively on commercial properties, combining hands-on field experience with organized project management to deliver predictable, professional outcomes on every roof we touch.

Our mission is simple: protect your building and your business with roofing solutions that are safe, durable, and financially responsible. We operate on three core values—transparency, craftsmanship, and reliability. That means clear communication before, during, and after your project; meticulous attention to detail on every seam, penetration, and flashing; and showing up when we say we will, ready to solve problems, not create them. Whether you manage a single facility or an entire portfolio, Paducah Commercial Roofing is committed to being the long-term roofing partner you can trust.
